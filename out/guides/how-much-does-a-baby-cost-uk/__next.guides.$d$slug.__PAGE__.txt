But the real cost varies enormously depending on your choices — from buying everything new at premium prices to a more budget-conscious approach using second-hand items and hand-me-downs.</p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">This guide breaks down the costs so you can plan realistically.</p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><h2 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mt-8 mb-3">One-Off Costs: Essential Baby Equipment</h2></p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">Here's what you'll typically need before your baby arrives:</p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">The Basics</h3></p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">Here's what you can expect to spend on essential baby equipment, from budget-friendly to premium options:</p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Pushchair or travel system</strong> — budget £150-£250, mid-range £300-£600, premium £700-£1,500</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Car seat (Group 0+)</strong> — budget £50-£100, mid-range £100-£200, premium £200-£400. Never buy a second-hand car seat unless you know its full history</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Cot or crib</strong> — budget £50-£100, mid-range £100-£300, premium £300-£800</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Cot mattress</strong> — budget £30-£60, mid-range £60-£120, premium £120-£250. Always buy a new mattress for safety</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Moses basket</strong> — budget £20-£50, mid-range £50-£100, premium £100-£200</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Changing mat or table</strong> — budget £10-£30, mid-range £30-£80, premium £80-£200</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Baby bath</strong> — budget £10-£20, mid-range £20-£40, premium £40-£100</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Baby monitor</strong> — budget £20-£40, mid-range £50-£120, premium £120-£300</span></li></p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><strong>Budget total: £340-£650.</strong> <strong>Mid-range total: £760-£1,560.</strong> <strong>Premium total: £1,660-£3,750.</strong></p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Clothing for the First 3 Months</h3></p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">Babies grow incredibly quickly. For the first 3 months, you'll need approximately:</p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span>6-8 bodysuits/vests (newborn and 0-3 months)</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span>6-8 sleepsuits</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span>2-3 cardigans/jumpers</span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span>2-3 hats</span></li>

<li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span>Socks/booties</span></li></p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><strong>Budget approach:</strong> £50-£100 (supermarket brands, sales, second-hand)
<strong>Mid-range:</strong> £100-£250
<strong>Premium brands:</strong> £250-£500+</p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Feeding Equipment</h3></p><p class="text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><li class="flex items-start gap-2 ml-4"><span class="text-violet-400 mt-1">•</span><span><strong>Breastfeeding:</strong> Nursing bras, breast pads, muslins — approximately <strong>£50-£150</strong></span></li>
